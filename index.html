Hello, Savvato.

Savvato aims to provide a platform for software developers of all skill levels to gain real-world, resume-building experience. We use that platform to connect our most promising candidates with jobs.

Through free and low-cost mentoring services, we identify candidate skills, and mentor candidates with effective advice, challenges, and feedback. We develop software to identify matches between employer wants, and our candidate’s skills. When we find a great match, we work to get that candidate placed in a great job.

Mock Interview

Our Mock Interview allows you to experience a technical interview before it matters and to get the feedback you never get in actual interviews. You select the job title you are aiming for and optionally which parts of the job you want to focus on: from topics like programming language concepts, database design, and querying to systems design, design patterns, and more. As you interview, we build a profile of the skills you have and the skills you need to work on. Employers and recruiters will leverage this profile to identify the skills they are looking for, and our flagship software will identify opportunities for candidate / employer matches.

Mock Programming Job

The Mock Programming Job is an at-will “learn on the job” project in which candidates take on tasks, and work under professional standards, as they would in a real job. They do the research to implement solutions, and they receive feedback on their efforts. Our philosophy is that the best way to prepare for the job is to do the job. Participants gain real world experience they can speak to in an interview, a sharpened set of skills, and, most of all, the confidence that they can hit the ground running in their next opportunity.
