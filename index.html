Tribe App

Tribe is a trust-based platform that connects people who need things done with those who can do them—and those who can teach them how. It’s built to support quality over quantity, helping users build real reputations and find the right people, not just anyone. Entrepreneurs can grow income streams without quitting their day jobs. Mentors guide new talent and can be paid upfront or later when those mentees succeed. Customers use Tribe to find help they can trust—not through anonymous reviews, but through real-world connections. Whether starting a business or getting a ride across town, Tribe makes it easier to find the right person for the job and grow a personal brand in the process.
