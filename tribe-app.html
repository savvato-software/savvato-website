Tribe is a trust-based social network where users can:

  • Earn money doing what they already know how to do
  • Collaborate with others as a fractional member of money earning opportunities of various sizes.
  • Learn new skills from mentors and build toward meaningful work
  • Find quality regular folks who can do what you need done
  • Become trusted and recognized in your areas of interest. People can seek your seal-of-approval to boost their reputation.

  • Millions of Gen Z youth in the U.S. are not in education, employment, or training (link)
  • Freelancing and gig work are exploding, but marketplaces are crowded and impersonal
  • AI enables smarter reputation modeling and skill-matching at scale

‚úÖ Why Tribe Works

  • Entrepreneurs post opportunities that others can join and make themselves available to customers
  • Mentors get paid on their own terms, up front, or after the mentee succeeds
  • Customers find trustworthy help filtered through real-world relationships
  • Everyone can define their own skills and attributes. Other users can vouch for them. You decide which of those vouches are meaningful for you. This ensures everyone can define who they want to work with
  • A user's actions which are positive for the network increase a user's reputation. Other users can use this to inform which collaborations they make.
  • If you have skills that are in demand, you have high flexibility in how others can reach out to you, even getting paid per request, if you decide.

ü§î Why Will People Join and Use Tribe?

  • For the money. Whether earning directly through a Money Earning Opportunity or investing in skills for future payouts, Tribe helps people make it work.
  • For businesses and stores: Tribe is a low-maintenance way to support entrepreneurial customers and earn community loyalty.
  • To claim a space and build a brand: This is a greenfield social network. Early adopters can stake their claim and grow influence and authority.
  • To improve recommendations: Instead of depending on bot-manipulated online reviews, customers can rely on trust signals from their personal network.
  • For connection and purpose: Tribe makes it easier to find people who align with your values and goals, whether you're looking to work, learn, or mentor.
